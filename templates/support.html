Privacy & Encryption
How is my data encrypted?
Your data is encrypted locally using the Web Crypto API. We use AES-256-GCM for file content and PBKDF2 with 200,000 iterations for key derivation from your password. This ensures your keys never touch the internet.
What does "Zero-Knowledge" mean?
It means Privacy Cloud cannot access your data even if we wanted to. Your password is never sent to our servers; it is only used as a seed for the encryption engine in your local browser environment.
Storage & Performance
Is there a file size limit?
Standard accounts support files up to 2GB. For files larger than 500MB, we recommend using a stable connection as browser-based encryption requires significant memory resources.
Will encryption slow down my uploads?
While encryption adds a processing step, our engine is highly optimized using your device's hardware acceleration via the Web Crypto API, resulting in near-native speed.
